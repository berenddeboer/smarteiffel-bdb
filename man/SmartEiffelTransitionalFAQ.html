Questions and answers
 
Q01: What is SmartEiffel (transitional) compared to official SmartEiffel?
A01: SmartEiffel (transitional) is a branch from official SmartEiffel. They are separate projects, developed by separate teams. However, both teams are communicated and share they work to avoid duplication of effort.
SmartEiffel (transitional) is developed from SmartEiffel 1.1, and intends to be 100% compatible with SmartEiffel 1.1, while allowing at the same time to write code that can run in SmartEiffel 2.x.
If you want further information about official SmartEiffel, check their website.
 
Q02: Is there a SmartEiffel (transitional) mailing list?
A02: Yes. You can find details about it in the project website.
 
Q03: How do I keep informed about SmartEiffel (transitional)? 
A03: You can keep on eye on our project website. We will keep news there about general progress.
The timeline that you can find there has automatically generated information about detailed changes. Also, you can keep track of development discussions at our wiki and mailing list.
 
Q04: How can I help?
A04: Glad you asked! We need help. Check our How to Contribute page, we keep some tasks there that you can help (even if you are not developer). If you want to get your hands into the code visit our Get Involved page.
It's important for our project to get involved with the community, and integrate other developers and their ideas.

   Questions and answers


   Q01: What is SmartEiffel (transitional) compared to official
   SmartEiffel?
   A01: SmartEiffel (transitional) is a branch from official SmartEiffel.
   They are separate projects, developed by separate teams. However, both
   teams are communicated and share they work to avoid duplication of
   effort.
   SmartEiffel (transitional) is developed from SmartEiffel 1.1, and
   intends to be 100% compatible with SmartEiffel 1.1, while allowing at
   the same time to write code that can run in SmartEiffel 2.x.
   If you want further information about official SmartEiffel, check
   their [6]website.

   Q02: Is there a SmartEiffel (transitional) mailing list?
   A02: Yes. You can find details about it in the [7]project website.

   Q03: How do I keep informed about SmartEiffel (transitional)?
   A03: You can keep on eye on our [8]project website. We will keep news
   there about general progress.
   The [9]timeline that you can find there has automatically generated
   information about detailed changes. Also, you can keep track of
   development discussions at our [10]wiki and [11]mailing list.

   Q04: How can I help?
   A04: Glad you asked! We need help. Check our [12]How to Contribute
   page, we keep some tasks there that you can help (even if you are not
   developer). If you want to get your hands into the code visit our
   [13]Get Involved page.
   It's important for our project to get involved with the community, and
   integrate other developers and their ideas.
